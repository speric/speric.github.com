Projects


TalentSoup

A marketplace connecting talent (actors, actresses, models) with producers of advertising imagery. Still in active development.

Tech: Rails 3 (upgraded from 2.3), MySQL, RSpec, Test::Unit, Apache + Phusion Passenger, Chargify for recurring billing, Amazon S3 for image hosting, Graphite + statsd for analytics.

Creative Commissary

Project management for photo producers. My first attempt at a multi-tenant architecture (ala Basecamp). Still in active development.

Tech: Rails 3, MySQL, nginx, RSpec, Stripe for recurring billing, Graphite + statsd for analytics.

SD News

A Hacker News clone for the Christian community to discuss trends in theology, apologetics, and church planting. Not currently maintained.

Tech: Rails 3, MySQL, nginx, Twitter and bitly gems for auto-posting popular stories.

Normative Press

Quotes from books I'm reading. A mixture of manual posts, and quotes from my Kindle highlights. Still in active development.

Tech: Rails 3, MySQL, nginx, kindle-highlights gem.

Sensus Divinitatis Publishing

A publishing company in the Scottish Calvinist tradition. We published The Kingdom Has Drawn Near: Studies In The Gospel Jesus Preached, and are working on another volume.

Tech: MS Word, Lulu, WordPress

kindle-highlights

Gem used to scrape Amazon's Kindle site for highlights I've made. Still in active development.

Tech: Ruby, Mechanize gem

Trinity Church Q&A

We wanted the youth of Trinity Church to be able to anonymously text questions to us during Q&As, so I built a small Sinatra app to parse emails from our inbox, and save only the content of the email to our database before destroying the original email. Still in active development, though the site is probably offline right now.

Tech: Sinatra, nginx, MySQL

cut-sheets

Parser to turn QuickPen-generated CSVs into useful pipe fabrication sheets. Still in development, and used almost daily.

Tech: Ruby

Rivendell Craft Brewery

The enjoyment of craft beer taken a little too far.

Tech: Water, barley, hops, yeast, fire, time.

Perplexed Labs, Inc.

Development blog and consulting services, in partnership with snakes.
