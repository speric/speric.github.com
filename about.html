Eric was born, raised, and schooled in New York. You can find him on Twitter or send him an email.

Eric is the technical co-founder and lead developer at TalentSoup. In addition to technical pursuits, he enjoys taking photos, listening to music and reading. All of these are enjoyed in the context of his family of three precocious children, and his beautiful wife.

Eric is also an elder at the Orthodox Presbyterian Church of Franklin Square and serves on the commission of Trinity Church in Huntington, New York.
